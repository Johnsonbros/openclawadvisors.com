Johnson Bros Plumbing: 4 AI Agents Running a Service Business

How a 2-person plumbing company in Greater Boston built an AI operations team

4 AI Agents Running 24/7
40+ AI Decisions Per Day
~$250 Monthly Cost

The Challenge

Two brothers running a full-service plumbing company. 50+ active jobs, constant customer communication, equipment research, scheduling coordination ‚Äî all while physically doing the work. Evenings and weekends were consumed by admin tasks.

The Solution

A 4-agent OpenClaw deployment, each agent with a specialized role:

  • ZEKE (Operator): Handles all communications, scheduling, customer follow-ups, and daily briefings
  • Huginn (Researcher): Researches equipment issues, competitor pricing, and business opportunities on demand
  • Muninn (Memory): Maintains institutional knowledge, cross-references all projects, prevents things from falling through cracks
  • Odin (Architect): Strategic planning, system design, and complex decision analysis

The Results

  • Admin time cut from 3+ hours/day to under 30 minutes
  • Zero missed customer follow-ups since deployment
  • Research tasks that took hours now completed in minutes
  • Morning briefings ensure nothing starts the day forgotten
  • Total cost: less than one service call per month
